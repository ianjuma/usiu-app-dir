Who we are

Benchcare is cloud-based, health record management (HRM) application modeled towards easing physicianâ€™s workflow. Our unique medical office software focuses on efficient administration and operation of small practices predominantly. With the aim of maximizing productivity, our system integrates patient record management, data import and export services as well as ordering and tracking of laboratory tests.

As part of our comprehensive solution, the software also includes a customizable personal public profile for the physicians or the Small Medium Enterprises (SME). The fundamental objective of our software is to deliver a fast, easy-to -use resolution for everyday administration difficulties. Our target market is Physicians and Small Medium Enterprises (SME) in the medical field. This will definitely have a big impact on the daily workflow and increase efficiency.

why choose benchcare

our responsibility

We hope to positively impact society by reducing the inefficiency and tedious nature of medical administration to allow for increased focus on quality patient healthcare.

The reduction of redundancies in patient records and flaws in data entry not only reduce the likelihood of errors but also heightens the attention available for patient needs and improving their experience.

As the health care industry becomes more competitive, and the players are required to increase their standards, we believe that Benchcare will become a very popular choice for physicians and small medium businesses. We strongly believe this will prove to be a very worthwhile and profitable investment in the future of digital healthcare.
